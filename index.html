Start Here

Follow these steps to compete effectively in NammaRust.

  1. Sign in with your Google account.
  2. Read monthly challenges by difficulty.
  3. Build and push your Rust solution on GitHub.
  4. Write a strong technical explanation.
  5. Share your work on LinkedIn.
  6. Submit through the form and track leaderboard updates.

Tip: early and high-quality submissions usually score better.
